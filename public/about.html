My Perspective

Strategy shaped by real life, not just theory.

My path has never been linear, and that has become one of my strengths. Military life taught me how to adapt quickly, listen carefully, and build trust in new communities. Communications taught me how to shape a message. Business school taught me how to connect that message to goals, decisions, and measurable outcomes.

Today, I bring that perspective into marketing work for nonprofits, healthcare brands, churches, events, and community-focused organizations. I care about the strategy behind the campaign, the clarity of the story, and the people on the other side of the message.
